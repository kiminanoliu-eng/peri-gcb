TRIO Panel Formwork

TRIO 框式模板  ·  Encofrado TRIO  ·  TRIO Rahmenschalung
The universal TRIO wall formwork ensures simple shuttering procedures and significantly reduced shuttering times. With just 6 standard panel widths and the unique BFD Coupler for all panel connections, TRIO has proven itself across thousands of projects worldwide — from residential buildings to infrastructure.

通用型 TRIO 框式模板专为简化支模操作、缩短施工周期而设计。仅需 6 种标准宽度模板，配合 BFD 连接件，适用于从住宅建筑到市政工程的各类墙体施工。
  • Only 6 standard panel widths — fewer components, faster logistics / 仅6种宽度，物流高效
  • BFD Coupler for all connections — no tools needed / BFD连接件，免工具操作
  • 100% compatible with MAXIMO system / 与MAXIMO系统完全兼容
  • Max concrete pressure: 80 kN/m² / 最大混凝土压力 80 kN/m²
  • Panel heights: 270 cm, 300 cm / 模板高度: 270cm、300cm
